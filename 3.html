Monstera 몬스테라

also known as the Swiss Cheese plant,
is a species of flowering plant native to tropical forests of southern Mexico
, south to Panama.

It has been introduced to many tropical areas,
and has become a mildly invasive species in Hawaii,
Seychelles, Ascension Island and the Society Islands.
